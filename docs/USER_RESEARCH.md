# Blog Module - User Research

**Module:** Blog  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Product Team

---

## Research Goals

1. Understand content preferences
2. Identify reading habits
3. Validate content topics
4. Determine engagement drivers

---

## Research Questions

| ID | Question | Priority |
|----|----------|----------|
| RQ1 | What topics interest users most? | P0 |
| RQ2 | How do users discover content? | P0 |
| RQ3 | What drives comments/shares? | P1 |
| RQ4 | Preferred article length? | P1 |

---

## Methodology

- **Surveys:** 200+ readers
- **Analytics:** Reading behavior analysis
- **Interviews:** 10 engaged readers
- **A/B Tests:** Headlines, formats

---

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: Educational Content Preferred
Users want to learn about prediction markets.

### Finding 2: Length Flexibility
Both short (500 word) and long (2000+ word) articles perform well.

### Finding 3: Visual Content Important
Articles with images get 2x engagement.

### Finding 4: Comments Drive Loyalty
Commenters have 3x retention rate.

---

## Recommendations

### Immediate
- Focus on educational content
- Include images in all articles
- Encourage comments

### Long-Term
- Build content recommendation engine
- Create video content
- Develop author community
