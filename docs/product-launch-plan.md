# Blog Module - Product Launch Plan

**Module:** Blog  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Product Team

---

## Launch Objectives

1. **Product:** Launch blog with 10 quality articles
2. **User:** Drive 1K monthly readers in first month
3. **SEO:** Index all articles in Google
4. **Engagement:** Generate first 50 comments

---

## Pre-Launch Checklist

### T-8 Weeks
- [ ] Content strategy defined
- [ ] Editorial calendar created
- [ ] Author guidelines written

### T-6 Weeks
- [ ] Publishing platform built
- [ ] SEO features implemented
- [ ] Comment system ready

### T-4 Weeks
- [ ] 10 launch articles written
- [ ] Design reviewed
- [ ] Analytics configured

### T-2 Weeks
- [ ] Go/No-Go decision
- [ ] Promotion plan ready

### T-1 Week
- [ ] Final content review
- [ ] Social posts scheduled
